# SaaS Portfolio Ideas for Small Business

## High-Impact Project Categories

### Operations & Management
- Appointment/booking system (service businesses love this)
- Inventory management dashboard
- Simple CRM with customer history and follow-up reminders

### Finance & Admin
- Invoice generator with payment tracking
- Expense tracker with receipt uploads
- Recurring billing / subscription management tool

### Customer-Facing
- Review aggregator (pull from Google/Yelp, display on site)
- Loyalty program / punch card app
- Simple e-commerce with local pickup support

### Communication & Marketing
- Email campaign tool with open-rate analytics
- Automated SMS appointment reminders
- Social post scheduler

## What Makes These Portfolio-Ready

- **Multi-tenant architecture** — shows you understand SaaS fundamentals (one codebase, isolated data per tenant)
- **Subscription/billing integration** — Stripe is the standard; knowing it is table stakes
- **Role-based access** — owner vs. employee vs. customer views
- **Mobile-responsive** — small business owners often work from phones

## Practical Advice

Pick **2-3 projects** rather than many shallow ones. Depth matters more than breadth. For each:
1. Deploy it publicly (Vercel, Railway, etc.)
2. Write a short case study — what problem it solves, tech choices, challenges
3. Include a live demo with realistic seed data

The booking system + CRM combo covers ~80% of what small service businesses actually ask for, so those two alone would make a strong foundation.
