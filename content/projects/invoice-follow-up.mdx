## The Problem

Small businesses lose significant time chasing unpaid invoices. Sending manual follow-up emails is tedious, easy to forget, and uncomfortable — so many just don't do it. Late payments hurt cash flow.

## The Solution

Invoice Follow-Up automates the reminder cycle. Connect your invoices, set a follow-up schedule, and let the tool handle the rest — professional, timely emails sent on your behalf until the invoice is paid.

## Key Features

- **Automated follow-up scheduling** — set intervals (e.g. 3 days, 7 days, 14 days after due date)
- **Customisable email templates** — personalised reminders that don't feel like spam
- **Invoice status tracking** — see at a glance what's outstanding, overdue, or paid
- **Stop on payment** — follow-ups automatically stop once an invoice is marked paid
- **Resend-powered delivery** — reliable email delivery with open tracking
