## The Problem

Many small businesses still invoice via Word documents emailed as PDFs. Tracking whether invoices have been viewed or paid requires manual follow-up. Late payments hurt cash flow.

## The Solution

A simple invoice tool: create an invoice, send a link, let the client pay online via Stripe, and get notified automatically.

## Key Features

- **Invoice builder** — line items, quantities, tax rate, due date
- **Shareable payment link** — clients open a hosted page and pay via Stripe Checkout
- **Status tracking** — Draft → Sent → Viewed → Paid → Overdue
- **Automatic receipts** — Resend fires a receipt email to both parties on payment
- **PDF export** — download a PDF copy for records

## Technical Decisions

Stripe Checkout handles the payment flow — no card data stored or processed server-side. Invoice status is updated via Stripe webhooks. PDF generation uses `@react-pdf/renderer` for server-side rendering.
